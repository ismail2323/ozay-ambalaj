Hışır

Crinkle packaging is an environmentally friendly packaging material made from crinkle paper or kraft paper that protects sensitive and fragile products against impacts. Thanks to its flexible structure, it fills the gaps around the product and prevents damage that may occur during transportation. Crinkle packaging offers both product safety and environmental awareness together.

  • • Material: Crinkle/kraft paper
  • • Usage areas: Electronics, glass, ceramics, gift products and e-commerce packaging
  • • Protection: Protection against impact, scratches and pressure
  • • Environmentally friendly: Recyclable and sustainable material
  • • Size and thickness options: Custom packaging solutions for products
  • • Lightweight and easy to use: Can be used in manual or automatic packaging systems
